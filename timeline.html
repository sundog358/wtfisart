Art Through Time

A chronological journey through art history and digital innovation of Sun & Rain Works

2005

Sun & Rain Works Founded

Establishment of Sun & Rain Works as a contemporary art business focused on Metaconceptual Art.

September 9, 2006

Early Metaconceptual Art Development

Key milestone in the development of Metaconceptual Art theory and practice at Sun & Rain Works.

Summer 2007

Metaconceptual Art Movement Founded

The Metaconceptual Art movement was founded, pioneered, and defined as "Art about Art" with 8 defining characteristics, including the return of Formless to Form, examination of art commodification, and transcendence of medium to arrive within collective consciousness.

Source: Metaconceptual Art
2008

WTF is Art Website Launch

Creation of "WTF is Art" as a Metaconceptual Art piece and platform for digital art exploration, in addition to "WTF is Ansel Adams", the other first Meta art work created live at auction at Sotheby's NYC, the actual auction happening itself in New York City.

March 30, 2009

Sotheby's Auction

"WTF is Ansel Adams" - Historic auction of previously undiscovered Ansel Adams photograph with live Meta art creation.

2015

Artistic Evolution

Expansion into digital art forms and interactive experiences.

September2019

Meta Art meets Meta Science.

Sun & Rain Works attended and participated in the first ever Metascience symposion at Stanford University in California. Art about Art meets and participates with Science about Science.

2025

Present Day

Continuing to explore and work in the frontiers of digital art from 2008 - 2005 with "WTF is Art", although this website has transferred ownership over the years since it's origi in digital art and technology with "WTF is Art" in 2025 and into the future.
